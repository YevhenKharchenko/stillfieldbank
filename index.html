Idea Behind the Game

A Puzzle Built on Simple Satisfaction

Fill the Piggy Bank started with a simple thought: what if organizing coins could feel like solving something meaningful?
It's not one of those crazy, fast-paced games. Just you, some coins, and figuring out where they go.
We kept it simple - no bells and whistles, just the satisfying challenge of making everything fit.
Each level is like a little puzzle where you've got to think it through but also trust your gut. You’re not just placing coins, you’re shaping a solution.

How It Works

There’s always a solution.

Gameplay Feel

Calm, Focused, Surprisingly Addictive

This isn’t a loud puzzle game. It doesn’t rush you. Instead, it builds tension in a quieter way — through limited space, careful placement, and the slow realization that one wrong move can block everything. And then, when it works — when everything aligns — it feels right.
It's weirdly addictive - stressful enough to keep you engaged but chill enough that you don't want to throw your phone.

Level Progression

Small Boards, Big Decisions

Levels begin with simple layouts that help you understand the rules. But very quickly, the game starts to shift.
More coins. Less space. Unexpected shapes. Tricky sequences.
You'll start seeing patterns, then realize they don't always work, then find even better ways to solve things.
Every new level asks a slightly different question.
