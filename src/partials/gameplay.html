Gameplay Feel

Calm, Focused, Surprisingly Addictive

This isn’t a loud puzzle game. It doesn’t rush you. Instead, it builds tension in a quieter way — through limited space, careful placement, and the slow realization that one wrong move can block everything. And then, when it works — when everything aligns — it feels right.
It's weirdly addictive - stressful enough to keep you engaged but chill enough that you don't want to throw your phone.
