Fill the Piggy Bank
One Coin at a Time

Stack, plan, and watch your piggy bank overflow

There’s something quietly satisfying about placing the last coin exactly where it belongs. We turned that into Fill the Piggy Bank - it's a puzzle game where every little move actually matters way more than you'd think.
At first, it feels simple. A few coins. A few slots. But then the space tightens, the sequence matters, and every move becomes part of a bigger solution.
