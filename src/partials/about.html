Idea Behind the Game

A Puzzle Built on Simple Satisfaction

Fill the Piggy Bank started with a simple thought: what if organizing coins could feel like solving something meaningful?
It's not one of those crazy, fast-paced games. Just you, some coins, and figuring out where they go.
We kept it simple - no bells and whistles, just the satisfying challenge of making everything fit.
Each level is like a little puzzle where you've got to think it through but also trust your gut. You’re not just placing coins, you’re shaping a solution.
